Target Audience

The target audience will be the people who are currently living in, visiting, or planning to be in this list of cities that we will be providing in this activity

Two personas

  1. Dan - 34 - construction worker - single father - moved to Preston to be closer to family. He also likes to experience the four seasons with Fall being his favorite. His two kids Jenna and Danny like to build snowmen during the winter months.
  2. Esther - 80 - married for 68 years to her high school sweetheart Donald, is planning to visit her grandkids in Soda Springs. She is wanting to surprise them for Christmas but since her kids just moved, she's not sure what the weather will be like.

Scenarios

  • How accurate is this website?
  • How easy is it to navigate?
  • Can I see this on my phone?
  • Does it have a 10 Day forecast option?
  • Will it have a Current Location option in case I'm not sure where I am?
